# Velanova — Narrated Walkthrough Script

### _"What is this platform and what can it do for you?"_

> A plain-English guide for a non-technical audience.  
> Intended for a screen-share demo, sales call, or recorded video.

---

## SCENE 1 — The Landing Page

**[Website opens at velanova.app — dark, professional homepage]**

> _"Welcome. What you're looking at is Velanova — an AI Adoption and Governance Engine. Don't let that name put you off. In simple terms: most companies are sitting on top of enormous amounts of data locked away in old systems — HR software, accounting platforms, customer databases, government systems. You can't easily ask questions of that data. You certainly can't have a conversation with it. Velanova changes that._
>
> _The headline says it best — 'Transform Legacy Systems into AI Powerhouses'. That's exactly what this does."_

**[Scroll slowly through hero section]**

> _"Notice the little demo window on the right side of the screen. A user types a plain English question: 'Show me top 10 customers by revenue this quarter.' Velanova connects to an Oracle ERP system, runs the query automatically, and returns the answer — in under one second. No SQL knowledge. No spreadsheet. No waiting for a report from IT. Just ask, and get the answer._
>
> _Two trust badges float alongside it — '10 million queries processed' and '99% uptime'. This is a production-grade platform, not a prototype."_

>
> _You add Velanova on top of whatever you're already using. Nothing moves, nothing breaks. You just suddenly gain the ability to talk to all of it."_

## SCENE 3 — Key Features

**[Scroll to Features section]**

> _"Here's a quick tour of what the platform can do:_
>
> _First — '64+ System Connectors.' That's your data sources — compatible with 95% of enterprise software in use today._
>
> _Second — 'Multi-Model AI Engine.' Velanova doesn't lock you into one AI. It can route your questions through ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, Meta's Llama, Mistral — whichever is best for your use case, or whichever you already have a contract with._

>

## SCENE 4 — Sign In

**[Navigate to Sign In page]**

> _"Now let's step inside the platform. You access Velanova through a desktop application that runs on Windows, Mac, or Linux. When you open it, you're greeted with a clean sign-in screen._
>
> _You log in with your company email and password — or your organisation may have single sign-on set up. Once authenticated, your session and settings are tied to your account, so you can pick up exactly where you left off."_

## SCENE 5 — The Library

**[Navigate to the Library page inside the desktop app]**

> _"This is the Connection Library — think of it as a catalogue of every type of system Velanova can talk to. It's organised into categories along the left._
>
> _Let me show you two specific ones that matter for a lot of our customers."_

### Finance & Markets Tab

**[Click 'Finance & Markets' category]**

> _"This is the Finance and Markets category. You can see dedicated connectors for Bloomberg Terminal — the gold standard for live market data. Reuters 3000. Murex MX.3, which is the trading and risk management platform used by major investment banks. Calypso for cross-asset trading. FrontArena. SimCorp Dimension._
>
> _For a bank or asset manager, this means a portfolio manager can open the chat window and ask: 'What is our current exposure to European government bonds across all active books?' — and get a real answer, pulling directly from Murex or Calypso, in seconds. No analyst intermediary. No waiting."_

**[Also click Finance category]**

> _"And within the broader Finance tab there's FIS, Finastra, Temenos for banking core systems, BlackLine for financial close management, and QuickBooks for smaller businesses. Whatever your finance stack looks like, it's covered."_

### Government Tab

**[Click 'Government' category]**

> _"Now the Government section. This is built for public sector organisations — councils, agencies, ministries._
>
> _CGI Momentum — a widely used government ERP platform. Tyler Technologies — used by hundreds of local and state government offices across North America for permits, court management, and financial operations._
>
> _Imagine a government finance officer asking: 'Show me all outstanding invoices over 60 days across all departments' — and getting that answer instantly from their live CGI system, without filing a data request or waiting for a report to be run manually."_

## SCENE 6 — Adding a Connection

**[Click a connector tile, e.g. Salesforce, to show the Add modal]**

> _"When you want to connect a system, you click its tile. A configuration form appears — it asks for the details needed to connect: a server address, your username, your API key or OAuth credentials. Your IT team fills this in once, and from that point on every authorised user on the account can query that system._
>
> _Once connected, it appears in your personal 'My Connections' list — you only ever connect a system once."_

---

## SCENE 7 — The Chat Window

**[Navigate to the Chat page]**

> _"This is the heart of the platform — the Chat window._
>
> _On the left, you have your conversation history. Every chat you've ever had is saved here, searchable, so you can go back to any answer you got last week or last month._
>
> _In the centre, the main chat area. This is where you type your questions._
>
> _At the top right, you choose which of your connected data sources this conversation is about — for example, you might pick your Salesforce connection, or your Oracle database, or your Bloomberg Terminal._
>
> _Let me walk through what a real conversation looks like."_

**[Type a question in the chat box]**

> _"Say I work in finance and I type: 'What were our top five revenue-generating products in Q4, compared to Q3?' — Velanova reads that question, understands what I'm asking, translates it into the right query for whatever database is connected, runs it, and returns a formatted answer right here in the chat. It even shows the SQL it ran, so technical users can verify exactly what happened._
>
> _Or if I'm in government procurement I might ask: 'List all contracts expiring in the next 90 days with a value over £50,000.' Same process — plain English in, structured data out._
>
> _The answers come back as formatted text, tables, even code blocks — whichever is cleanest for the type of data. You can copy any answer to your clipboard, or export it."_

---

## SCENE 8 — Model Selection

**[Click the model selector dropdown at the top of the chat window]**

> _"One of the most powerful features is right here — the model selector. This dropdown lets you choose which AI brain is answering your questions._
>
> _You'll see options from multiple providers:_
>
> - _OpenAI — GPT-4o, GPT-5, and the reasoning models like o3_
> - _Anthropic — Claude 3.7 Sonnet, Claude 3.5_
> - _Google — Gemini 2.0 Flash_
> - _Meta — Llama 4 and Llama 3_
>
> _Why does this matter? Different AI models are better at different things. GPT-4o is excellent for general business questions. Claude is particularly strong at reading long documents and contracts. Gemini Flash is very fast for high-volume routine queries. Llama models can be run entirely offline on your own hardware — important for organisations where data cannot leave the building._
>
> _You switch models with a single click. Mid-conversation. No restart required. And if you have your own API contracts with any of these providers, you bring your own keys and the cost goes directly to your existing agreement — Velanova doesn't mark it up."_

---

## SCENE 9 — Context Manager & Tools

**[Show the paperclip / context icon in the chat bar]**

> _"Notice the paperclip and context icons in the chat bar. These let you give the AI additional context for a conversation._
>
> _You can attach a file — a PDF contract, a spreadsheet, a policy document — and then ask questions about it alongside your live database data. 'Does this supplier contract comply with our procurement policy?' — with the contract attached and your procurement database connected, Velanova can cross-reference both at once._
>
> _The context manager also shows you exactly what information the AI has access to for this conversation — which database schema it knows about, what files are attached, what connection is active. Full transparency, full control."_

## Presenter Notes

| Tip                             | Detail                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Best live demo moment**       | Open the chat window, select a connected system the audience recognises, type a plain English question in real time. Seeing the answer arrive in under a second does more than any slide. |
| **Finance audience**            | Lead with Bloomberg / Murex connectors and the portfolio exposure question.                                                                                                               |
| **Government audience**         | Lead with CGI Momentum / Tyler Technologies and the outstanding invoices example.                                                                                                         |
| **Non-technical audience**      | Spend extra time on Scene 7. Keep the chat question simple and relatable. Avoid mentioning SQL.                                                                                           |
| **Security-conscious audience** | Emphasise Scene 8 (Bring Your Own Keys) and the offline Llama option.                                                                                                                     |
| **Length**                      | Full script runs approximately 12–15 minutes. Scenes 1–4 + 7–8 give a tight 5-minute version.                                                                                             |
